Good to Know

Visa tips & important info

⏰
Apply 3–4 months early
Most visas take 2–6 weeks to process. Apply well in advance and always check processing times at the official embassy website.
💰
Show sufficient funds
Every visa requires proof of financial stability. Have your bank statements ready for the last 3 months showing consistent income or savings.
🏫
DTV Soft Power tip
For Thailand DTV, enroll in a certified cooking school or Muay Thai gym for 6+ months. Get the official acceptance letter before applying.
🇬🇪
Georgia — easiest start
For most African and Middle Eastern passports, Georgia is visa-free for 1 year. Perfect first step to build your online income before tackling stricter destinations.
⚠️
Rules change often
Visa rules change frequently. Always verify at the official embassy website before submitting. LifeUnlocked Pro sends you real-time alerts when rules change.
💼
Digital Nomad proof
Some visas require proof of remote work. Freelance invoices, Fiverr/Upwork profile screenshots, bank transfers, or a remote work contract all count.
