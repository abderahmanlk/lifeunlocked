📋 The Complete Program

From $0 to immigration
in 4 phases

The only program that takes you from zero income to living in your dream country — with full support at every stage. No shortcuts. No false promises. Just a real plan that works.

Phase 01
🌱
Build Your First Income
Timeline: 0–3 months · Goal: $500–1,000/month

You start from zero — no experience, no clients, no savings in this account. We show you exactly which skill fits your profile, where to offer it, and how to get your first paid client within the first 2 weeks. Step by step, nothing skipped.

1
Choose your income source based on your skills and available hours (quiz result guides this choice)
2
Learn the tool: CapCut for video editing, Canva for design, or Claude AI for writing — all free, learn in 30 days
3
Create your profile on ComeUp (French market) and/or Fiverr (international) with a professional bio and portfolio
4
Offer your service for free to 3 people in your network to collect reviews and build your portfolio
5
Get first paying client · Deliver exceptional quality · Request a 5-star review · Repeat and raise your prices
Phase 1 Goal
$500–1,000/month online · Stable first income
Phase 02
💰
Scale to a Stable Salary
Timeline: 3–9 months · Goal: $2,000–5,000/month

You have your first income. Now we help you multiply it — more clients, better prices, automated workflow, and a second income source. This is where most people give up. We won't let you.

1
Raise your prices by 30% after your first 10 positive reviews — you've earned it
2
Add Upwork or Malt to reach European clients who pay 2–3x more than local markets
3
Start a second income stream: if you do design, add social media management; if video editing, start a faceless YouTube channel
4
Use AI tools (Claude, ChatGPT, Canva AI) to deliver faster and take more clients at the same time
5
Set up Payoneer + Wise to receive income internationally without restrictions · Build your runway to 3 months of expenses saved
Phase 2 Goal
$2,000–5,000/month · Consistent and growing
Phase 03
📋
Prepare Your Immigration
Timeline: 6–12 months · Goal: Visa approved + move date set

You have the income. Now it's time to prepare the move — legally, logistically, and financially. We handle the complexity so you don't make expensive mistakes.

1
Choose your destination based on your passport, income level, and lifestyle preferences (our visa checker guides this)
2
Prepare visa documents: bank statements, school/gym enrollment letter, accommodation proof — start 4 months before planned move
3
Set up international banking: Wise account, Payoneer, and research local bank options in your destination country
4
Research housing: Facebook groups for expats, Airbnb for first month, then negotiate long-term locally
5
Get international health insurance (SafetyWing from $45/month) · Buy your flight ticket 3–4 months in advance for the best price
Phase 3 Goal
Visa in hand · Flight booked · Housing arranged
Phase 04
✈️
Land & Install
Timeline: 12–18 months · Goal: Fully installed in your dream country

You arrive. And you're not alone. We help you navigate the first weeks, find your community, set up your finances, and start actually living the life you worked for — not just surviving in a new place.

1
First week: Airbnb or short-term rental while you explore neighborhoods · Don't sign a long-term lease before seeing in person
2
Open a local bank account within the first week (Bangkok Bank, Kasikorn, or CIMB depending on destination)
3
Get a local SIM card with good data plan · Set up your co-working space access · Test your internet setup
4
Join local expat groups on Facebook, Meetup, and Telegram · Find your community within the first 2 weeks
5
Continue working, growing income, exploring your new home · You made it. This is the beginning, not the end.
Phase 4 Goal
Installed · Earning · Living the dream 🌴
